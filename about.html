Hello, I'm Anna!

My name is Pham Phuong Vy, also known as Anna or Ennu. I’m a Digital Media student currently studying at RMIT University based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

As a newbie designer passionate about creating, I’m still on the journey of expanding my creativity and design abilities. I love building things in the digital world.


My creative skill set spans across 2D, 3D, and UI/UX, yet, my true fascination lies in 3D design, motion graphics, and animation. There’s something incredibly captivating about crafting movement, depth, and atmosphere, breathing life into static visuals and making them feel immersive and dynamic.

For me, design is all about balance. Sometimes it’s wild, playful, and experimental, and other times it’s super neat, organized, and precise. I like working with both sides, because I think creativity really happens when curiosity meets a bit of discipline. That mix shapes not just how I design, but also how I look at the world.

Outside of learning and creating, I love diving into music, games, films, and TV shows. Dancing is a big part of me too, and picking up new languages feels like collecting Pokémon cards. Oh, and I’m chronically online, so chances are if it’s on the internet, I’ve already seen it!
